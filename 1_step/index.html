Resulting global level:

The sound pressure levels of n non-correlated radiating sources characterized with pressures $p_i$ Pascal is \[ L_\Sigma = 10 \log_{10}\left(\frac{p_1^2 + p_2^2 + \dots + p_n^2}{p_0^2}\right)~\text{dB SPL} \] \[ L_\Sigma = 10 \log_{10}\left[\left(\frac{p_1}{p_0}\right)^2 + \left(\frac{p_2}{p_0}\right)^2 + \dots + \left(\frac{p_n}{p_0}\right)^2\right]~\text{dB SPL} \] Using: \[ \left(\frac{p_i}{p_0}\right)^2 = 10^{L_i/10},\quad i = 1, 2, \ldots, n \] We get: \[ L_\Sigma = 10 \log_{10} \left(10^{L_1/10} + 10^{L_2/10} + \dots + 10^{L_n/10} \right)~\text{dB SPL} \]
